    About Us

    Ticky.com an online bus ticketing portal. It brings together all private sector transport unions in Ghana under a single window, thus facilitating users to plan their bus travels ahead of the departure time to reduce the time wasted in vehicles and overcrowding With TICKY, you can book a bus online, to travel anywhere in Ghana. Being the only portal for inter-town connectivity, Ticky.com has a highly sophisticated system that enables users to do trip booking, seat reservation and hire buses.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to reduce passenger wait-time and overcrowding at lorry station in Ghana and making it possible for passengers to book tickets online to avoid inconveniences.

    Our Plan

    Our plan is to organize full trips starting from Ho to Accra and Kumasi. After two years, we aim to achieved a 50% coverage in Ghana by offering bus tickets in most cities and towns.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become the largest online bus booking platform in Ghana offering easy, Convenient, Affordable, Safe and Time bound travels.
